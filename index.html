How I Got into the Jailbreak Community?

I got into the Jailbreak community back when the iPhone 4 was just coming out in stores. I was in middle school and were hearing about how the newer iPhone 4S had Siri. I became curious and decided to do some research if it were possible to get Siri on an iPhone 4. I came across a Jailbreak tutorial using Redsnow on iOS 6. I followed the tut with no idea what the hell I was getting myself into. It soon came to the step where the Redsnow Pineapple showed up on the screen and started bursting through code down the screen. At that point, I started freaking out so bad, thinking I just screwed up my phone, my dads gonna be pissed at me, how am I gonna fix it?, etc. Turns out I just had to wait another 2 minutes for it to finsih and things were just peachy. Later that night, I got Siri sorta working and I realized what Jailbreaking was. I started tweaking and theming my device so much that everyone I went to school with was so confused and amazed at what I had. That started my Jailbreaking future.
